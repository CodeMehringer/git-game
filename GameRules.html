UH-OH Game Rules

Game Rules

  • 1. There are 4 player spots labeled with 4 different colors.
  • 2. Each player has 4 game pieces.
  • 3. Each player takes turns drawing cards until a 2, 12, or "UH OH" card is drawn.
  • 4. A 2 or 12 gets the player out of start and then it is the next player's turn.
  • 5. The "UH OH" can be used if another player has a game piece on the board that is not in a safety zone.
  • 6. When a player draws a card, the directions for that card will be displayed and the player chooses which game pice to move and moves it according to the directions.
  • 7. If it is not possible for a player to move any of their game pieces the turn is over and moves to the next player.
  • 8. If you land on another players game piece or run into it during a slide, that player' game piece returns to their start where a 2, 12 or sorry is needed to get out again.
  • 9. Players need to be able to take turns intermittently and be let known when it is their turn.
  • 10. Players can only enter the safe zones and home of their own color. If a player is in their safezone, their game piece cannot be removed and sent to start.
  • 11. The game is over when a player gets all 4 game pieces to HOME

What Do The Cards Do?

  1. Forward 1 spaces
  2. Get out of start or forward 2 spaces
  3. Forward 3 spaces
  4. Backwards 4 spaces
  5. Forward 5 spaces
  6. Forward 6 spaces
  7. Forward 7 spaces and draw again
  8. Forward 8 spaces or split the spaces between 2 of your game pieces
  9. Forward 9 spaces
  10. Forward 10 spaces or backwards 1 space
  11. Forward 11 spaces or switch spaces with another player
  12. Forward twelve spaces or get out of start
  13. UH-OH - Take a player from your start area and switch places with anyone on the board. If no players are on the board move out of start one space
